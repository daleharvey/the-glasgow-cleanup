Glasgow Cleanup Post Generator

This is a tool (by @daleharvey) to help generate image posts to share a time and location for a cleanup. To find the coordinates for a location visit OpenStreetMap, click the "Share" button on the right hand side of the screen and check "Include marker" checkbox, then place the marker over the desired location and copy the Geo Url (for example geo:55.8611,-4.2502?z=16). Then enter a date, time and location name. You can right click the resulting image and save to post on Instagram etc.
